About Me

I graduated from Full Sail University on July 2015 with a Bachelors of Science degree in Music Business. My education consists of: Artist Management, Concert Management and Publicity, Critical Listening for the Music Professional, Music Business Law and Contract Negotiations, Music Business Management, Music Business Marketing, Music Copyright and Publishing, Music Distribution, Music Merchandising and Retail Promotion, Music Supervision, and Record Label Development. I am the type of person that helps someone with their goals. I help artists by guiding them through the industry on how to publish themselves and to form a everlasting image. The music industry is a great place to live and learn. I am constantly learning and building myself to better my future.
